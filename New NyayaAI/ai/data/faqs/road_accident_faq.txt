Q: 2024 mein India mein kitne road accidents hue?
A: 2024 mein India mein 4,67,967 road accidents hue. In accidents mein 1,75,142 log mare aur 4,48,365 log injured hue. Yeh data NCRB ke Accidental Deaths & Suicides in India 2024 report ke anusar hai.

Q: 2023 mein India mein kitne road accidents hue?
A: 2023 mein India mein 4,64,029 road accidents hue jisme 1,73,826 log mare aur 4,47,969 log injured hue. 2024 mein road accidents 0.8% badhkar 4,67,967 ho gaye.

Q: 2024 mein road accidents mein sabse zyada maut kis state mein hui?
A: 2024 mein road accidents mein sabse zyada maut Uttar Pradesh mein hui jahan 22,923 log mare. Uske baad Tamil Nadu (18,449 deaths) aur Maharashtra (15,888 deaths) hain. Ye teeno states milkar total deaths ka 33% hissa hain.

Q: Road accidents ka sabse bada karan kya hai?
A: 2024 mein road accidents ka sabse bada karan overspeeding hai jo 61.2% accidents ke liye zimmedar hai. Overspeeding se 2,86,325 accidents hue aur 1,01,649 log mare. Doosra bada karan dangerous/careless driving ya overtaking hai jo 26% accidents ke liye zimmedar hai.

Q: 2024 mein kis vehicle se sabse zyada road accident deaths hue?
A: 2024 mein two wheelers se sabse zyada 84,599 deaths hue jo total road accident deaths ka 48.3% hai. Uske baad pedestrian (25,769 deaths - 14.7%) aur cars/SUV/jeep (23,739 deaths - 13.6%) hain.

Q: Road accidents kis time par sabse zyada hote hain?
A: Road accidents sabse zyada shaam 6 baje se raat 9 baje ke beech hote hain jo total accidents ka 20.8% hai. Uske baad dopahar 3 baje se shaam 6 baje ka time hai jo 17.4% accidents ke liye zimmedar hai.

Q: Road accidents kin mahino mein sabse zyada hote hain?
A: Road accidents sabse zyada May mahine mein hote hain — 2024 mein May mein 41,364 accidents hue jo total ka 8.8% hai. Tamil Nadu mein May mein sabse zyada accidents hote hain.

Q: National Highway par kitne accidents hote hain?
A: National Highways sirf 2.1% road network hai lekin 29.8% road accidents aur 34.8% deaths inhi par hote hain. 2024 mein National Highways par 1,39,433 accidents hue aur 60,894 log mare.

Q: Drunk driving se kitne accidents hote hain?
A: 2024 mein drunk driving se 1.7% road accidents hue jisme 3,375 log mare aur 7,435 log injured hue. Uttar Pradesh mein drunk driving accidents sabse zyada hain jo total ka 36.4% hai.

Q: Road accidents rural area mein zyada hote hain ya urban area mein?
A: 2024 mein 59.7% road accidents rural area mein hue (2,79,412 cases) aur 40.3% urban area mein (1,88,555 cases). Dono jagah sabse zyada accidents residential area ke paas hote hain.

Q: 2024 mein traffic accidents se total kitne log mare?
A: 2024 mein traffic accidents (road + railway) se total 1,99,443 log mare. Ismein road accidents se 1,75,142, railway track incidents se 22,413 aur railway crossing accidents se 1,888 deaths shaamil hain.

Q: Cities mein road accidents ki sthiti kya hai?
A: 2024 mein 53 major cities mein 73,426 traffic accidents hue jisme 17,797 log mare. Delhi mein sabse zyada 2,181 deaths hue, uske baad Jaipur (1,001 deaths) aur Bengaluru (894 deaths) hain.

Q: 2020 se 2024 tak road accidents mein kya trend raha hai?
A: 2020 mein 3,54,796 accidents the jo 2024 mein badhkar 4,67,967 ho gaye. Deaths bhi 1,33,201 se badhkar 1,75,142 ho gayi. COVID ke baad 2021 se accidents mein lagatar badhot dekhi gayi hai.

Q: Rajasthan mein 2024 mein kitne road accidents hue?
A: Rajasthan mein 2024 mein 24,119 road accidents hue jisme 11,444 log mare.

Q: Madhya Pradesh mein 2024 mein road accident ki sthiti kya hai?
A: Madhya Pradesh mein 2024 mein 55,711 road accidents hue jo sabse zyada accidents wale states mein se ek hai. Yahan 15,152 log road accidents mein mare.
