Q: Helmet na pehenne par kitna fine hai?
A: Helmet na pehenne par Rs 1000 fine lagta hai aur 3 mahine ke liye driving licence disqualify ho jata hai. Yeh Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 ki Section 194D ke antargat aata hai. Har vyakti jo motorcycle chalata hai ya pillion par baithta hai, dono ko helmet pehenna anivarya hai.

Q: Drunk driving par kitna fine hai?
A: Drunk driving par pehli baar pakde jane par Rs 10,000 fine aur/ya 6 mahine ki jail hoti hai. Doosri baar pakde jane par Rs 15,000 fine aur/ya 2 saal ki jail ho sakti hai. Yeh Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 ki Section 185 ke antargat aata hai. Blood Alcohol Content (BAC) 30mg per 100ml blood se adhik hone par yeh offence maana jata hai.

Q: Seat belt nahi pehni toh kitna fine lagega?
A: Seat belt na pehenne par Rs 1000 fine lagta hai. Yeh Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 ki Section 194B ke antargat aata hai. Driver aur saare passengers ko seat belt pehenna anivarya hai. 14 saal se kam umra ke bachche ke liye bhi seat belt ya child restraint system zaroori hai, warna Rs 1000 fine lagega.

Q: Overspeeding par kitna fine hai?
A: Overspeeding par light motor vehicle ke liye Rs 1000 se Rs 2000 tak fine lagta hai. Heavy vehicle ke liye Rs 2000 se Rs 4000 tak fine lagta hai. Doosri baar pakde jane par driving licence impound ho sakta hai. Yeh Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 ki Section 183 ke antargat aata hai.

Q: Bina driving licence ke gaadi chalane par kya hoga?
A: Bina driving licence ke gaadi chalane par Rs 5000 fine lagta hai aur 3 mahine tak ki jail ho sakti hai. Yeh Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 ki Section 181 ke antargat aata hai.

Q: Mobile phone use karte hue gaadi chalane par fine kya hai?
A: Mobile phone use karte hue gaadi chalane par Rs 1000 se Rs 5000 tak fine lag sakta hai. Yeh dangerous driving ke antargat aata hai aur Section 184 ke antargat punishable hai. Pehli baar 6 mahine jail aur Rs 1000 se Rs 5000 fine, doosri baar Rs 10,000 fine.

Q: Red light todne par kitna fine hai?
A: Red light todna dangerous driving ke antargat aata hai. Iske liye Rs 1000 se Rs 5000 tak fine lagta hai. Yeh Section 184 ke antargat aata hai.

Q: Bina insurance ke gaadi chalane par kya hoga?
A: Bina insurance ke gaadi chalane par pehli baar Rs 2000 fine aur/ya 3 mahine jail hoti hai. Doosri baar Rs 4000 fine aur/ya 3 mahine jail ho sakti hai. Yeh Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 ki Section 196 ke antargat aata hai. Har vehicle ka third party insurance hona anivarya hai.

Q: Hit and run accident mein kitna compensation milta hai?
A: Hit and run accident mein mrityu hone par Rs 2 lakh compensation milta hai. Gambhir chot lagne par Rs 50,000 compensation milta hai. Yeh Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 ki Section 161 ke antargat aata hai. Motor Vehicle Accident Fund se yeh compensation diya jata hai.

Q: Accident mein Good Samaritan ko kya protection hai?
A: Good Samaritan woh vyakti hai jo accident victim ki madad karta hai bina kisi inam ki ummeed ke. Unhe kisi bhi civil ya criminal case mein liable nahi rakha ja sakta. Police unhe baar baar interrogate nahi kar sakti. Yeh Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 ki Section 134A ke antargat protected hai.

Q: Minor ne gaadi chalai toh kiske upar case hoga?
A: Agar minor (18 saal se kam) ne gaadi chalai toh guardian ya vehicle owner guilty mane jayenge. Guardian ko 3 saal jail aur Rs 25,000 fine lagega. Minor ka driving licence 25 saal ki umra tak nahi milega. Vehicle ka registration 12 mahine ke liye cancel ho jayega. Yeh Section 199A ke antargat aata hai.

Q: Driving licence kitne saal ke liye valid hota hai?
A: 30 saal se kam umra ke liye licence 40 saal ki umra tak valid hota hai. 30 se 50 saal ke liye 10 saal ke liye valid hota hai. 50 se 55 saal ke liye 60 saal ki umra tak valid hota hai. 55 saal se upar ke liye 5 saal ke liye valid hota hai. Yeh Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 ki Section 14 ke antargat aata hai.

Q: Learner licence ke liye kya karna hota hai?
A: Learner licence ke liye kisi bhi State ke licensing authority mein apply kar sakte hain. Online ya offline dono tarike se apply kar sakte hain. Theory test dena hota hai. Learner licence 6 mahine ke liye valid hota hai. Is dौraan L plate lagana anivarya hai.

Q: Driving licence suspend kab hota hai?
A: Pehli baar traffic offence mein 3 mahine ke liye suspend ho sakta hai. Doosri ya baar baar offence mein revoke ho sakta hai. Overspeeding, drunk driving, dangerous driving mein suspend ho sakta hai. Suspend period mein driver refresher training course karna anivarya hai. Yeh Section 19 ke antargat aata hai.

Q: Overloading par kitna fine hai?
A: Overloading par Rs 20,000 fine lagta hai aur Rs 2000 per tonne extra load ka fine lagta hai. Gaadi ko tab tak rok liya jayega jab tak extra load nahi utara jata. Yeh Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 ki Section 194 ke antargat aata hai.

Q: Emergency vehicle ko rasta nahi dene par kya hoga?
A: Ambulance, fire brigade ya emergency vehicle ko rasta nahi dene par Rs 10,000 fine aur 6 mahine jail ho sakti hai. Yeh Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 ki Section 194E ke antargat aata hai.

Q: Bina seat belt ke child ko gaadi mein le jane par kya hoga?
A: 14 saal se kam bachche ke liye seat belt ya child restraint system nahi lagane par Rs 1000 fine lagta hai. Yeh Section 194B ke antargat aata hai.

Q: Traffic police ko document nahi dikhane par kya hoga?
A: Traffic police maange toh driving licence, registration certificate, insurance certificate, pollution certificate dikhana anivarya hai. Nahi dikhane par fine lag sakta hai. 7 din ke andar police station mein dikhane ka mauka milta hai. Yeh Section 158 ke antargat aata hai.

Q: Vehicle ka speed limit highway par kya hai?
A: Central Motor Vehicles Rules 1989 ke antargat highway par car ke liye maximum speed limit 100 kmph hai. Trucks aur buses ke liye 80 kmph hai. State Government apne hisaab se speed limit kam kar sakti hai. School zone aur residential areas mein speed limit 25-30 kmph hoti hai.

Q: Road sign kya hote hain aur kitne prakar ke hote hain?
A: Road signs teen prakar ke hote hain. Pehla mandatory signs jo red border ke saath hote hain aur inhe follow karna anivarya hai jaise stop sign aur no entry. Doosra cautionary signs jo yellow background ke saath hote hain aur khatre ki warning dete hain. Teesra informatory signs jo blue background ke saath hote hain aur direction ya information dete hain.

Q: Pollution under control certificate kya hai?
A: Pollution Under Control (PUC) certificate ek document hai jo vehicle ke emission levels verify karta hai. Yeh certificate gaadi chalate waqt saath rakhna anivarya hai. Petrol vehicle ke liye har saal aur diesel vehicle ke liye har 6 mahine mein renew karna hota hai. Bina PUC ke gaadi chalane par fine lagta hai.

Q: Third party insurance kya hota hai aur kyon zaroori hai?
A: Third party insurance ek mandatory insurance hai jo accident mein dusre vyakti ko hua nuksan cover karta hai. Bina third party insurance ke koi bhi vehicle public place mein nahi chala sakta. Yeh Motor Vehicles Act 1988 ki Section 146 ke antargat anivarya hai. Accident mein mrityu hone par Rs 5 lakh aur gambhir chot par Rs 2.5 lakh compensation diya jata hai Section 164 ke antargat.
